Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about The Azure Pearl. If you can't find what you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact our team.

Booking & Reservations

How can I make a reservation? +
You can make a reservation through our website by clicking the "Book Now" button, calling our reservations team at +1 (305) 555-0199, or emailing reservations@azurepearl.com. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Cancellations made 48 hours or more before your check-in date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in are subject to a one-night room charge. Special rates and packages may have different policies.
Can I modify my reservation? +
Yes, reservations can be modified up to 24 hours before check-in, subject to availability. Please contact our reservations team or use your confirmation number to make changes online.
Do you require a deposit? +
We require a credit card to guarantee your reservation. A deposit equal to one night's room rate will be charged at the time of booking. The remaining balance is due upon check-in.

Check-in & Check-out

What are your check-in and check-out times? +
Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.
Can I request early check-in or late check-out? +
Yes, early check-in and late check-out can be requested. Based on availability, early check-in may be available from 12:00 PM and late check-out until 2:00 PM for an additional fee. Please contact our front desk.
What do I need to bring for check-in? +
Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID and the credit card used for booking. International guests should also have their passport ready.

Hotel Amenities

Is WiFi included? +
Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the hotel, including all guest rooms, public areas, and outdoor spaces.
Do you have parking available? +
Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all guests. Our secure underground parking garage ensures your vehicle is safe during your stay.
What dining options are available? +
The Azure Pearl features two award-winning restaurants, a rooftop bar, a poolside cafe, and 24-hour room service. We offer everything from fine dining to casual fare.
Is there a fitness center? +
Yes, our state-of-the-art fitness center is open 24/7 and features modern equipment, free weights, and personal training services by appointment.

Rooms & Services

Are pets allowed? +
Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel. We welcome dogs and cats under 25 pounds. A non-refundable pet fee of $75 per stay applies. Please notify us in advance.
Do rooms have mini bars? +
Yes, all rooms are equipped with premium mini bars stocked with beverages and snacks. Suites feature enhanced mini bar selections and Nespresso machines.
Can I request extra amenities? +
Absolutely! We offer additional amenities including extra pillows, robes, towels, cribs, rollaway beds, and more. Please contact our concierge for special requests.
Is smoking allowed in rooms? +
All guest rooms are non-smoking. We have designated outdoor smoking areas on our terrace and pool deck. A $300 cleaning fee applies if smoking occurs in rooms.

Payment & Policies

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and cash. Payment is required at check-in.
Are there any additional fees? +
Room rates do not include a daily resort fee of $35, which covers WiFi, fitness center access, pool amenities, and morning coffee service. Local taxes apply as per state regulations.
Do you offer group rates? +
Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 10+ rooms. Please contact our group sales department at groups@azurepearl.com for customized packages and pricing.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help 24/7. Contact us anytime for personalized assistance.
